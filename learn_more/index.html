Overview

CogniTriv is a prep tool designed to provide an engaging and efficient way to prepare for quiz competitions. With access to over 200,000 questions across diverse categories, it allows users to sharpen their knowledge, practice recall, and build confidence through repetition and challenge-based learning.

Why is this useful?

Using CogniTriv gives you hands-on experience with real question formats, helping you become more comortable under pressure. It's not just about memorization--it's about broadening your horizons, reinforcing your strenghts, and improving your response accuracy through practical engagement.

How it works

  1. Press Start
  2. Answer each question as they appear
  3. Press the Finish to complete the quiz
    • Note that, you need to answer at least one and there is no limit to how many you can answer
  4. Press Reveal to see your perfomance
